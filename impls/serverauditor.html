Remarks

  • Mobile client for Android as well as iOS (iPhone, iPad).
  • There is also a beta of a Desktop version, implemented as a Google Chrome plugin.
  • Available at the Apple App Store, Google Play, Google Chrome web store.
  • 2015-07-10: Version 2.0.0 for iOS identifies itself on the wire as SSH-2.0-libssh2_1.4.3, so likely based on libssh2.
