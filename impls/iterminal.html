Remarks

  • Client for Apple iOS (iPhone, iPod, iPad).
  • Available at the Apple App Store in three versions: iTerminal iTerminal Pro for iPhone and iTerminal Pro for Universal
  • 2015-10-30: Version 2.02 for iOS identifies itself on the wire as SSH-2.0-libssh2_1.4.3, so likely based on libssh2.
