Remarks

  • Mobile client for Android and iOS.
  • Available at Apple App Store, Google Play.
  • They also offer clients and servers for Unix and Windows, under the name Reflection for Secure IT (formerly known as F-Secure SSH). These should be covered by a separate entry.
  • 2015-07-10: Version 1.3.1.21 for iOS identifies itself on the wire as SSH-2.0-libssh2_1.4.3, so likely based on libssh2.
